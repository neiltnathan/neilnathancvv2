About me

Profile

My Name is Neil Nathan. I am a (web) developer and expatriate from London located in The Hague, The Netherlands and up until recently I worked as a Lotus Notes Developer and Domino Server Administrator for a large multi-national company located in Amsterdam, providing Domino Server support, including maintenance & upgrades as well as developing applications both internally and externally for its corporate customers.

Highlights & Achievements

My main achievement during my most recent period of employment was the development of a Lotus Notes / Web Survey Application (Customer Satisfaction Survey Robot). This application was originally developed by me for the EMEA Internal Helpdesk (circa 120K users) in 2000. Since then, it continued to be developed by me, for the regional internal Helpdesks & Commercial Customer Account Service Desks and supported by me until 2016.

To protect the company’s intellectual property rights the application was registered with a patent attorney. An early legacy version of the application was also purchased (under licence) by a corporate customer. Since 2005 the application became the Global Strategic Survey Tool for the company service desks.

I was also the EMEA focal & an administrator for the survey application. I travelled throughout the US, Canada & Europe providing education and demonstrating the survey application to corporate customers and customer account management teams as well as providing advice and consulting on customer satisfaction and survey tools/processes when required. I was also responsible for requirements gathering and estimating costs of proposed new features. As a Speaker (x2) at The Lotus / IBM Best Practices Conferences in Cambridge & Boston (US) I presented/demonstrated the survey application.

A small global team was also created as the survey applications success grew with new team members in the US and India.

At its peak the survey application was being used globally by over 100+ concurrent corporate customers, issuing 5+ million surveys per year with one of the best survey return rates in the industry. The survey application had inbuilt Root Cause Analysis functionality and survey results were available in real-time allowing customer accounts to be pre-emptive in managing the customer satisfaction of their service desks.

As can be seen I take ownership and I am determined to see assigned projects or tasks through to a successful completion. I also enjoy finding creative solutions to challenging problems and I work well alone or within a team.

Access Management Request Application

Description:

The Access Management Request Application was developed at the request of a corporate customer to automate the access requests to Lotus Notes applications, to document (audit) the request processes and to eliminate as many manual process steps as possible.

Environment:

Windows Server, Linux Server, Lotus Domino Server, Lotus Notes database

Key Features:

  • A fully automated Lotus Notes access request application for end users to request different levels of access to one or more Lotus Notes applications.
  • End users can request access to a single or multiple Lotus Notes applications
  • Notifications are sent to application owners upon access request for approval
  • Upon approval access is granted autmatically to the requesed application
  • An audit log records all access request activity.

Key Achievements:

  • Cost effective.
  • Efficient handling of requests

Archive Application

Description:

The Lotus Notes Archive application was developed at the request of a corporate customer to help manage the number of Lotus Notes applications in production.

Environment:

Windows Server, Linux Server, Lotus Domino Server, Lotus Notes database, tape backup.

Key Features:

  • A fully automated Lotus Notes archiving application
  • Archive application monitors change of archive status for each application
  • Upon an archive request the archive process is initiated
  • After 30 days the application is automatically transferred to tape backup
  • Archived application is automatically removed from production server
  • An audit log records all access request activity.

Key Achievements:

  • Cost effective.
  • Fully automated
